About

Meet Devin Patrick Gaughan.

I am a computer programmer located in Portland, Oregon. I studied Computer Science in the Pacific Northwest United States. I have worked on Firmware Engineering teams at HP, Intel, and Xerox. Most of my experience involves 2D and 3D printer technology. I have worked in hardware servicing with Fujifilm and Epson products. I experiment with a Prusa i3 3D printer at home to advance my knowledge of print technologies.
